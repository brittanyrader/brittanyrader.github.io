Welcome! I'm Dr. Brittany Rader and I'm an Assistant Professor at Midland University. I earned my PhD in mathematics from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln in May 2023 with a research concentration in mathematics education. My research focuses on the goals and beliefs held by Graduate Teaching Assistants and how goals an beliefs impact how they decide to support their students.
