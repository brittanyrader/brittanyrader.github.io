Teaching Experience

Teaching at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln (2018-2023)

During my time at UNL, I taught the following courses (listed in reverse chronological order).

  • Math 301 - Geometry Matters (Spring 2023)
  • Math 300 - Math Matters (Fall 2022)
  • Math 301 - Geometry Matters (Spring 2021)
  • Math 103 - College Algebra and Trigonometry (Fall 2021)
  • Math 301 - Intermediate Algebra (Fall 2019 through Spring 2020)
  • Math 106R - Calculus I Recitation (Summer 2019)
  • Math 107R - Calculus II Recitation (Spring 2019)
  • Math 106R - Calculus I Recitation (Fall 2018)

In addition to teaching these courses, I served at the Associate Convener for Intermediate Algebra during the 2020-2021 academic year. As the associate convener, I worked closely with the faculty course convener to oversee the Intermediate Algebra course. This involved creating assessments, tracking student progress, and running weekly meetings for the 9 instructors for the course. I also provided general support to the instructors (many of whom were first time instructors of record) on a day-to-day basis.
